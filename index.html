About

Vegapunk Semiconductor, Inc. is a fabless logic chip design company headquartered in Austin, TX, supplying embedded controllers to automotive and industrial clients across North America. Founded in 2018, Vegapunk operates on a direct-to-OEM sales model with a 12–18 month design cycle. FY 2025 marked the company's first full year of volume production on its V3 logic controller platform — the primary driver of revenue growth and variance activity throughout the year.

Management Commentary

Quarterly Variance Analysis — FY 2025 Prepared by FP&A  ·  Vegapunk Semiconductor
Q1 2025 — Jan / Feb / Mar−$4.4M vs plan

Revenue missed plan by $4.4M as the V3 logic controller platform ramped slower than projected. Wafer allocation constraints delayed customer shipments in January and February. March partially recovered on expedited orders, but Q1 closed below target on both revenue and EBITDA.

Q2 2025 — Apr / May / Jun+$3.8M vs plan

Q2 recovered strongly as supply constraints resolved and a major automotive OEM accelerated a June shipment pull-in worth $3.3M above plan. April and May saw continued softness before the pull-in order drove a clean quarterly beat on both revenue and EBITDA margin.

Q3 2025 — Jul / Aug / Sep+$3.8M vs plan

Strong industrial sector demand drove Q3 above plan on revenue. EBITDA lagged budget as OpEx increased from a planned engineering hire cohort onboarding in July. The revenue beat was partially offset by higher personnel costs, compressing margins through the quarter.

Q4 2025 — Oct / Nov / Dec−$1.7M vs plan

October and November missed plan as key distribution customers executed inventory corrections ahead of year-end. December recovered sharply with a $3.8M beat driven by the industrial holiday order cycle and a new design win shipment at a Tier-1 automotive client. Full Q4 closed slightly below plan despite the strong December finish.
